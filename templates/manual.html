2. Dashboard

The dashboard is your home screen. It shows:

  • KLCI Sentiment — Current KLCI index price, daily change, market mood (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral), and trend direction. Helps you gauge overall market conditions before buying.
  • Summary Cards — Total stocks scanned, number of BUY/WATCH/SELL signals at a glance.
  • Top 5 Picks — The highest-scoring stocks from the latest scan.
  • Last Scan Time — Shows when data was last refreshed and how long ago (e.g. "2h 15m ago").
💡 Tip: Click "Run Scan" to fetch fresh data. Scan results are cached for 24 hours and persist across app restarts.

3. Scanner

The main scanning page displays all analyzed stocks in a sortable, filterable table.

Filters

  • Signal buttons — Filter by All / BUY / WATCH / SELL signals
  • Presets — One-click filters: Growth (score ≥30 + BUY), Dividend (yield ≥4%), Momentum (RSI ≥50, ADX ≥20, Vol ≥1.5x), Penny (<RM1)
  • Search — Type any symbol or company name
  • Sector dropdown — Filter by sector (Technology, Consumer, etc.)
  • Price range — Set min/max price in RM

Sorting

Click any column header (Score, Price, RSI, ADX, Vol, Div%, Risk) to sort ascending/descending. The ▲▼ arrow shows current sort direction.

Pagination

Results show 50 per page by default. Use Prev/Next buttons or change to 100, 200, or Show All from the dropdown.

Table Columns

Score Net score from weighted indicators (-100 to +100). Higher = more bullish
RSI Relative Strength Index (14-period). Below 30 = oversold, above 70 = overbought
ADX Average Directional Index. Above 25 = strong trend, below 15 = no clear trend
Vol Volume ratio vs 20-day average. Above 1.5x = unusual activity
Div % Trailing 12-month dividend yield. 💰 shown when ≥4%
Risk Risk level: Low / Medium / High based on volatility, RSI, volume, trend

4. Stock Detail

Click any stock row in the scanner to open its detail page. Here you'll find:

  • Interactive Chart — Candlestick chart with EMA 20/50/200, Bollinger Bands, volume bars, MACD, and RSI sub-charts.
  • Technical Indicators — Full breakdown of all computed indicators with current values.
  • Signal Analysis — Detailed BUY/SELL reasons explaining why the score was assigned.
  • Support & Resistance — Key price levels identified from recent price action.
  • Entry Plan — ATR-based stop-loss (price − 2×ATR), take-profit (price + 3×ATR), and risk/reward ratio.
  • Position Sizing — Suggested lot size based on RM10,000 capital and Bursa's 100-share lot.
  • Risk Score — Detailed risk assessment with specific warnings.

5. Sectors

Shows sector rotation analysis across 11 Bursa Malaysia sectors (Technology, Consumer, Industrial, Property, Finance, etc.). Each sector displays average performance, number of BUY signals, and trending direction. Use this to identify which sectors are leading/lagging the market.

6. Volume Spikes

Lists stocks with unusual volume activity (significantly above their 20-day average). Volume spikes often precede major price moves — they can signal institutional buying/selling, news catalysts, or breakouts. Sorted by volume ratio (highest spike first).

💡 Tip: A volume spike combined with a BUY signal is a strong confirmation. Check the stock detail page for full analysis.

7. Portfolio

Track your actual stock holdings with real-time P&L.

How to use

  1. Enter the stock symbol (e.g. MAYBANK or 1155.KL)
  2. Enter your buy price (RM) and quantity (number of shares)
  3. Click "Add to Portfolio"

What you'll see

  • Risk Summary Cards — Total positions, average P&L, capital at risk, sell/high-risk alerts
  • P&L % — Percentage gain/loss since your buy price
  • Stop-Loss — Suggested stop-loss level (2× ATR below current price)
  • Risk Level — Per-holding risk badge
  • Current Signal — Latest signal for each holding (watch for SELL signals!)

8. Watchlist

Track stocks you're interested in without buying yet. Set price targets to get visual alerts.

  • Target High — Set an upper price target. When the stock reaches this price, a 🔔 alert badge appears.
  • Target Low — Set a lower price target for buying opportunities.
  • Notes — Add your own notes (e.g. "Wait for breakout above RM2.50").

9. Signal Tracker

Tracks how well BUY signals performed over time. After a BUY signal is generated, the tracker checks the actual stock price at 7, 14, and 30 days later.

  • Win Rate — Percentage of signals that were profitable at each time period
  • Average Return — Mean return across all tracked signals
  • Best/Worst — Top and bottom performing signals
💡 Tip: If the 7-day win rate is consistently above 50%, the scanner's signals are reliable. Below 50% means you should use additional confirmation before buying.

11. Understanding Signals

Signals are computed from a weighted scoring system:

EMA Trend +20 -20 Price above/below EMA 20/50/200
MACD +15 -15 MACD crossover signals
RSI +15 -15 Oversold/overbought momentum
ADX +15 -15 Trend strength + direction (DI+/DI-)
Volume +15 -5 Volume above average confirms move
52-Week +5 -10 Position within 52-week range
Bollinger +5 -5 Price near upper/lower band
Multi-Timeframe +10 -10 Weekly timeframe confirmation

Signal Thresholds

STRONG BUY: score ≥ 50 BUY: score ≥ 25 WATCH: score ≥ 10 HOLD: score > -10 SELL: score ≤ -10 STRONG SELL: score ≤ -30

12. Risk Management

Stock Hunter includes several tools to help minimize risk:

Risk Score

Each stock is rated Low / Medium / High based on:

  • ATR volatility > 5% of price (+3 points)
  • RSI > 75 overbought (+3 points)
  • ADX < 15 no clear trend (+2 points)
  • Volume < 0.5× average low liquidity (+2 points)
  • Near 52-week high (+3 points)

Score ≥5 = High risk, ≥2 = Medium, <2 = Low

Entry Plan (ATR-based)

  • Stop-Loss = Current price − (2 × ATR) — limits your downside
  • Take-Profit = Current price + (3 × ATR) — targets 1.5:1 reward-to-risk
  • Risk/Reward Ratio — Green if ≥1.5 (good), red if <1 (avoid)

⚠️ Risk Warnings on Scanner

Yellow ⚠️ icons appear next to each stock with specific warnings: overbought RSI, low volume, near 52-week high, high volatility, or no clear trend.
